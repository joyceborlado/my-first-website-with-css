About me

I am a licensed professional teacher who has recently discovered a strong interest in web development. Fascinated by the endless possibilities of the digital world, I am currently learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design and build engaging websites. My journey into web development has also included hands-on experience with WordPress, where I have successfully created several websites. This new interest allows me to explore my creativity and technical skills in a fresh and exciting way.

Balancing my passion for teaching with my enthusiasm for web development, I am excited to see how these two fields can complement each other. By integrating technology into education, I aim to create innovative learning experiences for my students. As I continue to expand my skills and knowledge, I look forward to discovering new ways to merge my expertise in teaching with my growing proficiency in web development.
